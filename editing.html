Editing

The majority of my editing work has been in the field of tabletop roleplaying games. Not only do TTRPG books have to read as cleanly as any other written publication, they also have to convey complex rules to readers at a variety of skill levels. I specialise in helping to craft clear, logically laid-out instructions that don't grate against themselves or the flow of their intended use.

As a developmental and substantive editor (or sometimes "design consultant", a type of developmental editing pertaining to game mechanics), I enjoy working closely with writers, making sure I have a thorough understanding of their intentions and helping them make sure those intentions are conveyed. I work flexibly at the micro and the macro level, able both to dig in deep and to look at the bigger picture, ensuring that every aspect of the work is clear.

That didn't stop Satah, who turned around edits under schedule with tons of suggestions. Dense, marked-up Google Doc. Love it.
