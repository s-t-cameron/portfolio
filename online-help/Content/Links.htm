Links

Linking uses portal keys to draw a line between two portals.

Creating Links

To link two portals, they must both be

  • Owned by your faction

  • Fully slotted with eight resonators

Linking to a portal requires having a key for it in your inventory. When you're within interaction distance of the portal you want to create the link from, tapping Link searches within your keys for potential valid links. Creating the link consumes that key.

After tapping Link, portals that could be linked to are highlighted pink in the scanner.

The portal being linked to must be within range of the portal being linked from. A portal can only support 8 outgoing links (unless modded with a SoftBank Ultra Link), but there are no limits on how many incoming links a portal can receive. Links cannot cross each other, regardless of faction.

Effects

Links mitigate some damage taken from enemy agent attacks, with diminishing returns for each additional link. When three linked portals form a triangle, it creates a control field.

Link Range

The range limit of a link is based off the resonators and mods on the portal being linked from. The resonators and mods on the portal being linked to has no effect.

It can be calculated using this formula:

Link Range (in meters) = 160 * (Average Resonator Level * 4) * Link Amp Amplification

Examples

Resonators Link Amp Link Range
R1 R1 R1 R1 R1 R1 R1 R1 1 Rare 1280 meters
R8 R8 R8 R8 R8 R8 R8 R8 1 SoftBank 25600 meters
R4 R4 R5 R5 R6 R6 R7 R8 2 Very Rare 31500 meters

Under Control Fields

Links between portals that are under a control field, regardless of faction, can only be 2000 meters long.
